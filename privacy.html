Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

At Reelance, we protect your personal information and value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, store, use, and share your data when you visit or interact with our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better connection experience for our users. This includes:

  • Account Registration Data: Your name, email address, password, and chosen profile role (Client or Creator).
  • Creator Listing Details: If you join as a creator, we collect your base rate, location city, portfolio details, bio description, categories, and core tags/skills. This information is publicly displayed.
  • Platform Communications: Inquiry messages (subject and body text) exchanged between clients and creators are securely stored on our servers.
  • Analytics Data: Standard server logs, IP addresses, and approximate geolocation data (used for the "Near Me" local search filter).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the creative marketplace.
  • To allow clients to discover and search for creators based on city location and specialization.
  • To power your user dashboard (Workspace and Inquiries Inbox).
  • To send platform notifications, including email confirmations and account updates.
  • To audit platform activity and protect against fraud, spam, or abuse.

3. Sharing and Disclosing Data

We believe in direct relationships. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following contexts:

  • Public Profile: If you are a verified creator, your name, bio, location, rate, and skills are visible to all logged-in users.
  • Direct Inquiries: When a client sends a message inquiry to a creator, their email address, name, and message details are shared with the recipient.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Reelance relies on secure, industry-standard third-party providers to power core services:

  • Supabase: Used for secure user authentication, session management, and database storage.
  • Resend: Used to deliver system and account verification emails.

These third parties process your data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Advertising & Cookies

We may use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally-identifiable information (such as your IP address, browser type, or visits to this and other websites) in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Specifically:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

6. Data Security and Deletion

We use SSL encryption and secure database controls to safeguard your data. You have the right to request deletion of your account. You can completely delete your profile by contacting administrator support, or adjust your creator listing availability at any time inside your dashboard workspace.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@codewithtechnyks.com.

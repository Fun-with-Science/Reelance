About Reelance

// commission-free connection for creative pros

Reelance was founded on a simple belief: creators should keep 100% of their earnings, and clients shouldn't pay bloated platform fees. Traditional freelance platforms act as gatekeepers, hiding emails, blocking portfolios, and charging up to 20% in commissions. We are here to skip that chaos.

Our Core Value Proposition

We connect vetted creative professionals directly with clients. No middleman, no escrow hold, no platform taxes. You discover talent, review portfolios, check rates, and message them directly. From there, you work together and pay however you want.

0% Commission-Free

We take zero commission from clients or creators. The rate creators list is the rate you pay.

DM Direct Contact

Message creators directly. Exchange emails, contract on your terms, and choose your own payment methods.

VT Vetted Talent

Every creator portfolio is verified for quality and authenticity before their listing goes active.

SF Direct Support

Get direct support. If you run into violations, fake accounts, or scams, report them immediately for prompt action.

How We Maintain Platform Quality

Because Reelance is a direct peer-to-peer marketplace, we take trust and safety seriously. Both clients and creators can be rated by users they interact with. Fake accounts, spammers, and malicious users are proactively flagged.

Administrators audit exchange logs and support inquiries. Violating profiles are immediately banned for a period of 2 to 3 months, or permanently, to ensure that the Reelance community remains clean, professional, and productive.

Want to get in touch?

Have questions, feedback, or need to report a user policy violation? Reach out directly via our contact form page.
